"Let's say that I am an Order, I would think I would want to know what items I have ordered possibly in the form of a Line Item.  If anyone wanted to know what the total of my line items were, I would expect them to ask me rather than ask for the line items and do the calculation itself."

This is often the way I think of an objects role...

While I might find this a good way to verbalize the roles and responsibilities for a class, it is difficult to use the same approach when it comes to visualizing the state of this system.  The idea that I have come up with is to replace the rectangular boxes in UML class diagrams with real world objects that represent the domain class....
